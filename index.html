Unfinished Thoughts

Hey there! I'm Jason Connor, and I have a confession: I'm terrible at finishing side projects. But that's not actually the point of them. Not really. Side projects are for playing with new technologies. They're where I can get my hands dirty and my head into new ideas. Completed projects are for my day job.

What I Do for a Living

By day, I'm a software engineer with a Master's in Computer Science. I geek out over algorithms, functional programming and concurrency, but what really gets me excited is building stuff that actually works. My go-to languages are Python and Golang, with a healthy dose of SQL and TypeScript when needed.

I'm a command-line junkie – Vim is my second home, and I probably spend too much time customizing my development environment. Recently, I've been having way too much fun integrating AI into my workflow. Check out some cool tools I've been playing with:

  • vim-codeium for code assistance
  • aider for pair programming with AI
  • Ollama, LiteLLM and Langfuse for playing with AI applications
